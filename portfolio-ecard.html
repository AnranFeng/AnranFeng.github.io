Ecard: Fluo Party invitation

This is a assignment of my multi-media design class. And it was produced by Flash.

The background image is a picture of balloons in a party, and I added some elements around the main text "have fun!" To make it looks funkier. Besides, there are 3 main effects of this e-card.
1) The flash of the text "have fun!", making it a Neon light, and also aiding the fluo party theme;
2) The motion and shape change of the little elements around the main text, making the whole card more dynamic. Each effect of them are different.
3) The motion of subtitle had used different effects.
